Mastocytosis (Systemic Mast Cell Disease)

Comprehensive peptide therapy targeting mastocytosis, a clonal mast cell disorder characterized by abnormal accumulation of mast cells in skin, bone marrow, gastrointestinal tract, liver, spleen, and lymph nodes. This protocol addresses: c-KIT (CD117) signaling inhibition to block mast cell proliferation, induction of mast cell apoptosis, inhibition of mast cell degranulation via SNARE complex blockade, tryptase and histamine blockade, mast cell membrane stabilization, cytokine storm suppression, and tissue protection from mast cell infiltrates. Targets both indolent systemic mastocytosis (ISM) and aggressive systemic mastocytosis (ASM), including mast cell leukemia variants.

1. Mast cell stabilization and anti-degranulation: Carnosine - Naturally occurring imidazole dipeptide; stabilizes mast cell membranes; inhibits PI3K-Akt phosphorylation; reduces IgE-dependent degranulation and histamine release; enhances IL-4 production for immune modulation; safe natural mast cell stabilizer
2. Mast cell receptor blockade and anti-allergic: MCD-Peptide - Mast cell degranulating peptide from bee venom; at high concentrations inhibits mast cell degranulation via disulfide exchange with IgE; blocks IgE binding to Fc{epsilon}RI; dual-action: low dose activation, high dose inhibition; anti-inflammatory at therapeutic doses
3. SNARE complex inhibition and degranulation block: SNARE-VAMP2 - VAMP2-derived 17-mer SNARE motif peptide; inhibits SNARE complex formation; prevents mast cell degranulation at molecular level; blocks vesicle fusion and mediator release; effective in atopic dermatitis models when fused with protein transduction domain
4. Neuro-immune mast cell regulation: VIP - Vasoactive intestinal peptide; modulates mast cell-neuron interactions; protects tissues from mast cell inflammatory damage; reduces systemic mast cell activation; stabilizes mast cells in neuro-immune axis; anti-inflammatory via cAMP/PKA pathway
5. Anti-inflammatory and NF-kB inhibition: Ac-KPV-NH2 - Alpha-MSH-derived anti-inflammatory tripeptide; inhibits NF-kB activation in mast cells; suppresses TNF-{alpha}, IL-6, and IL-8 production; reduces mast cell-mediated inflammation; neuroprotective against mast cell toxicity
6. Tissue protection and mast cell stabilization: BPC-157 - Cytoprotective pentadecapeptide; stabilizes mast cells in tissues; prevents stress-induced degranulation; accelerates healing of mast cell-mediated tissue damage; modulates NO pathway; protects organs from mast cell infiltrates
7. Antimicrobial and mast cell pathogen response: LL-37 - Cathelicidin-derived antimicrobial peptide; mastocytosis patients prone to infections; LL-37 provides broad-spectrum defense; modulates mast cell responses to pathogens; reduces infection-triggered mast cell activation
8. Neurogenic mast cell modulation: Substance-P - Eleven-amino-acid neuropeptide; modulates mast cell-neuron communication; at appropriate dosing can stabilize mast cell activity; supports tissue healing from mast cell damage; regulates neuro-immune mast cell axis
9. c-KIT/CD117 inhibition and mast cell proliferation block: KIT-Inhib-1 - Designed nonapeptide interfering with SCF/c-KIT signaling; blocks stem cell factor binding; inhibits mast cell proliferation and survival; reduces mast cell burden in tissues; complements imatinib/avapritinib mechanism
10. Mast cell apoptosis induction: MastApop-1 - Designed amphipathic cationic tetradecapeptide; selectively induces apoptosis in abnormal mast cells; disrupts mitochondrial membranes in proliferating mast cells; spares normal mast cells; reduces mast cell burden in mastocytosis
11. Tryptase inhibition and mediator blockade: TryptaseBlock-1 - Designed cationic tridecapeptide targeting beta-tryptase active site; blocks tryptase-mediated tissue damage; reduces PAR-2 activation; lowers vascular permeability and fibrosis from mast cell infiltration
12. Histamine receptor modulation: HistamineBlock-1 - Designed octapeptide modulating H1 and H4 receptors; reduces histamine signaling without complete blockade; alleviates flushing, pruritus, and hypotension; supports mast cell stabilizer function
13. Mast cell membrane stabilization: MastStab-Core - Designed cysteine-rich octapeptide with disulfide bridges; stabilizes mast cell membrane integrity; prevents calcium influx and degranulation; reduces mediator release without cytotoxicity; maintains mast cell homeostasis
14. Mast cell cytokine storm suppression: CytokineCalm-MC - Designed heptapeptide reducing mast cell cytokine overproduction; lowers IL-6, IL-8, TNF-{alpha} release; targets late-phase mast cell activation; stabilizes mast cell transcriptional response in systemic mastocytosis
