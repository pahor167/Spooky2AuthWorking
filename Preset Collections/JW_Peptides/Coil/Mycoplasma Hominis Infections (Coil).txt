7. Membrane permeabilization: Bactenecin - Bovine neutrophil peptide that disrupts membrane integrity
8. Adhesion inhibition (Vaa adhesin targeting): Vaa-Blocker (designed) - Synthetic peptide blocking Vaa adhesin to prevent epithelial attachment
9. Anti-inflammatory modulation: Cytokine-Mod (designed) - Peptide suppressing TNF-{alpha} and IL-6 production
10. Tissue regeneration: RGD-Healer (designed) - RGD-containing peptide promoting fibroblast adhesion and wound closure
