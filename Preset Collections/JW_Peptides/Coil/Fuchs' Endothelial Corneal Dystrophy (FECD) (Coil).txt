Comprehensive peptide therapy targeting Fuchs' endothelial corneal dystrophy, a progressive degenerative disorder characterized by loss of corneal endothelial cells, formation of guttae (collagenous excrescences on Descemet membrane), corneal edema due to pump failure, and vision impairment. Targets both early-stage (endothelial cell loss) and late-stage (stromal edema) disease progression.

1. Anti-fibrotic and endothelial protection: Thymosin-Beta-4 - 43-amino acid actin-sequestering peptide; prevents endothelial-mesenchymal transition (EndMT) in FECD; inhibits TGF-{beta}1-induced fibrosis; promotes corneal endothelial cell survival and migration; reduces guttae formation

3. Neuro-immune modulation and anti-inflammatory: VIP - Vasoactive intestinal peptide; protects corneal nerves and endothelial cells from inflammatory damage; reduces IL-6 and TNF-{alpha} in anterior chamber; maintains endothelial barrier function; anti-apoptotic via cAMP/PKA pathway
4. Endothelial regeneration and antioxidant: GHK-Cu - Copper-binding tripeptide; upregulates tissue repair genes in corneal endothelium; stimulates collagen synthesis in Descemet membrane; promotes endothelial cell proliferation; antioxidant via ROS scavenging
5. Inflammation suppression in anterior chamber: Ac-KPV-NH2 - Alpha-MSH-derived anti-inflammatory tripeptide; inhibits NF-kB activation in corneal endothelium; reduces cytokine-induced endothelial cell loss; prevents progression of FECD via anti-inflammatory action
6. Neurogenic inflammation and wound healing: Substance-P - Eleven-amino-acid neuropeptide; promotes corneal epithelial and endothelial wound healing via NK1R; enhances cell migration; supports endothelial pump function recovery; modulates neuro-immune responses in FECD
7. TGF-{beta} pathway inhibition and EndMT prevention: TGFbetaBlock-1 - Designed cationic undecapeptide with CPP motif; blocks TGF-{beta} receptor signaling; prevents endothelial-mesenchymal transition (EndMT) characteristic of FECD; maintains endothelial phenotype; reduces guttae and fibrosis
8. Nrf2 antioxidant pathway activation: Nrf2Activ-1 - Designed nonapeptide with disulfide-bonded cysteines; activates Nrf2/ARE pathway in corneal endothelium; upregulates antioxidant enzymes (SOD, catalase, GPx); protects against oxidative stress-induced endothelial cell loss in FECD
9. Endothelial pump function enhancement: PumpSupport-1 - Designed nonapeptide stabilizing Na+/K+-ATPase in corneal endothelium; enhances fluid pump function to reduce corneal edema; maintains endothelial cell ion homeostasis; compensates for pump failure in advanced FECD
10. Descemet membrane ECM stabilization: DescemetStab-1 - Designed octapeptide mimicking elastin-like repeats in Descemet membrane; stabilizes collagen VIII and XVIII networks; reduces abnormal ECM deposition (guttae formation); maintains membrane elasticity and transparency
11. Direct ROS scavenging in endothelium: EndoAntiOx-1 - Designed heptapeptide with redox-active cysteines; mimics glutathione peroxidase activity; directly scavenges ROS in corneal endothelium; protects against oxidative DNA damage and apoptosis in FECD progression
12. Multi-pathway endothelial protection: FuchsProtect-1 - Designed heptapeptide targeting multiple FECD pathways; inhibits apoptosis via caspase-3 blockade; reduces oxidative stress; maintains endothelial tight junctions; comprehensive protection for compromised endothelium
13. Descemet membrane collagen support: CollagenXVIII-1 - Designed octapeptide from collagen XVIII/endostatin sequence; supports Descemet membrane integrity; inhibits abnormal angiogenesis; stabilizes endothelial basement membrane in FECD; reduces guttae formation
14. ECM remodeling and MMP modulation: MMPmod-1 - Designed octapeptide modulating MMP activity in Descemet membrane; prevents excessive ECM degradation; balances matrix turnover in FECD; reduces abnormal collagen deposition; maintains optical clarity
