Dysregulated metabolism manifests as slowed basal metabolic rate, insulin resistance, impaired mitochondrial function, excessive fat accumulation, or inefficient energy utilization, leading to symptoms such as persistent fatigue, unintended weight gain or difficulty losing weight, cold intolerance, muscle weakness, brain fog, hormonal imbalances, and increased risk of metabolic syndrome, type 2 diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and non-alcoholic fatty liver disease. This ensures broad coverage of metabolic dysregulation, with designed sequences optimized for short length, high solubility, proteolytic resistance, and minimal immunogenicity, feasible for subcutaneous or oral delivery.
