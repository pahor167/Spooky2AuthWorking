The incubation period ranges from 2 to 12 days, after which patients experience an abrupt onset of high fever (often exceeding 39 C), severe and debilitating joint pain (arthralgia) that can affect multiple joints symmetrically, muscle pain (myalgia), headache, fatigue, nausea, and a maculopapular rash that may appear on the trunk and limbs.
