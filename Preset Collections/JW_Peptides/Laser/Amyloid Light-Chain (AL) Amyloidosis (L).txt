Causes stem from an underlying monoclonal gammopathy, often with low tumor burden (<10-20% plasma cells), though it can overlap with multiple myeloma in ~10-15% of cases; triggers for clonality remain unclear but involve somatic mutations and possible environmental/antigenic stimuli.
